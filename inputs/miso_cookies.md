MISO SESAME CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIE 2025 Edited: 3/25/26
Yields: 65 COOKIES
INGREDIENTS:
460 grams AP FLOUR
1.25 teaspoon BAKING SODA A
1.5 teaspoon BAKING POWDER
0.25 teaspoon SEA SALT
282 grams BUTTER, SOFT AT ROOM TEMP
396 grams ORGANIC CANE SUGAR
100 grams EGGS
10 grams SHIRO MISO (WHITE MISO, YAMABUKI BRAND)
50 grams DARK CHOCOLATE CHIPS
10 grams BLACK AND WHITE SESAME SEEDS
METHOD:
1 In large bowl, mix/whisk all dry ingredients "A" until well incorporated. Set aside.
2 In mixing bowl with paddle, on medium speed whip butter until soft and aerated.
3 Add sugar until well incorporated, scraping down sides of bowl.
4 Add eggs, one at a time and mix on low-medium speed until well incporated, scraping down sides of bowl.
5 Add miso and whip on low-medium speed until well incorporated, no chunks.
6 Add dry ingredients, and mix on low speed until just incorporated. DO NOT OVERMIX!
7 Refrigerate and let rest at least 20 min or overnight.
8 Preheat oven to 350f, high fan.
9 To assemble, scoop and portion dough 18g, and add chocolate disc or chocolate chips in between so chocolate is in middle.
10 Roll exterior of cookie in toasted sesame seeds
11 Flatten cookies to 1/2" height. They should be at 60f before baking. Not woo warm, not too cold.Sprinkle a pinch of sea salt on top of each cookie.
12 Bake 8 min until golden. Remember to rotate the tray half-way through baking time. Outside should be crisp, inside soft. Cool on rack.
13 Serve warm. If not serving immediately, hold in fish tub with parchment paper and reheat at 350f for 2 minutes to serve warm.
